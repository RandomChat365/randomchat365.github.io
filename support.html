Support

Need help? Have a question? Our support team is here to assist you. Please check our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section below, or feel free to reach out to us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I start a chat?

Simply click on the "Text Chat" or "Video Chat" button on the home page to begin connecting with a random stranger.

Is RandomChat365 free to use?

Yes, our basic chat features are completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future.

How can I report a user?

If you encounter any inappropriate behavior, please use the report function within the chat interface or contact us directly.

Contact Us

If your question isn't answered in the FAQ, please send us an email at: support@randomchat365.com

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.
